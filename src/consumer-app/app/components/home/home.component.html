Frequently Asked Questions

+ WHAT IS A “BID”?
When you find a product you like, some stores may offer the option for you to propose a price. Our technology will automatically determine if the price is acceptable and if the store agrees, it will sell this product to you for your bid price. And you can always submit a new bid if the store did not accept your first price.
